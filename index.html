Vision

Europe is diversity and culture, friendship and cooperation, sustainability and future. How faint-hearted to want to reduce the European idea to financial issues - no matter how important they may be! How faint-hearted it would be for us to call the European project into question rather than to imagine the next level.

Our people

The longest period of peace in the history of Europe as we know it has allowed that vision of a group of citizens in Aachen to become reality. But that success does not make the work of peace superfluous, even if we humans often think less of that which we have come to take for granted. You may know the story of the people who built dykes along the North Sea coast and those dykes were breached every four to five generations.

Our view

Europe's unique diversity and momentum has contributed a lot to the present state of world - in a positive sense but not only in a positive sense. Ultimately, the trend towards industrialisation and globalisation and, as a consequence, the ever faster pace of things and lack of boundaries have their roots in Europe. Globalisation and new technologies call for a new order, and Europe's skills and experience will be needed for that.
